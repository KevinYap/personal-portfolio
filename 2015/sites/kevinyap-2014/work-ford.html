Ford Interactive Screen

2012

Ford Focus launch campaign. I have to develop 3 panels design and the look & feel I pick is more like futuristic because this can bring out the car image itself and also the features of the car.

My role in this project was design the interactive screen and also animate how the features show out when user click on each of the usp of the car which is an orb surround the car.

Disciplines: Design Client: Ford Malaysia Skill used: Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash
