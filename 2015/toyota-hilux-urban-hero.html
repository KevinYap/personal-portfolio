Toyota Hilux Urban Hero

The Hilux Urban Hero is an online contest held by Toyota Malaysia. Inspired by the bold characteristics of the Hilux, the Urban Hero was born - The Urban Hero is defined as someone who has done a kind deed in your life or even for society (e.g. rushed to your aid when you were stranded in a car breakdown on the highway, played sports with the less fortunate kids, delivered food to flood victims, etc.) What's important is that no matter how big or small the deed, it has left a lasting impression in your life!
