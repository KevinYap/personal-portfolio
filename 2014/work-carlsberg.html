Carlsberg Footballism

2013

Carlsberg is now the official sponsor for Barclays Premier League for 2013, 2014, 2015. They would like to have a stronger online presence on web and to drive CLM platform around this idea of 'FOOTABALLISM' (football bible concept).

The campaign has 2 key objective, drive engagement of brand thru the BPL association and leading to consumption at event screening events or carlsberg joints. The second part which is the essentially the focal part of this campaign is to collect customer data for the CRM program.

My role in this project pitch was mainly design and construct the overall look & feel of the app and also think of the engagement between the consumer and the on-ground-event.

Disciplines: Design Client: Carlsberg Skill used: Photoshop, Illustrator
