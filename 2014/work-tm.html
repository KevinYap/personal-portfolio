Tokio Marine

2014

Based in Singapore, Tokio Marine Management Services Pte Ltd began providing insurance-related services to Tokio Marine and Fire Insurance Co. Ltd’s regional subsidiaries in 1992.

Revamp and unify the websites of in-scope TMA's Group Companies. The in-scope group companies are Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, Hong Kong and Australia. Besides that, it is to allow online quotation for Travel, Motor and Term Life Insurance for Singapore only. I was approached early on to design based on the given wireframes and the client requested home page should be beautiful, clean and organized platform for visitors to swiftly navigate.

Disciplines: Design Client: Tokio Marine Asia Skill used: Photoshop, Illustrator
